about REMOVING​ an app from google play

Hello fans of Genshin Impact & users of this app

The purpose of this application was and is to provide easy and quick access to promotional codes which appear on the Internet. Also, thanks to your requests and ideas, other functions have appeared.

Apparently, some users might have thought that this is an official application, but not. This is just the app from Genshin Impact fan. From one fan to other fans. Nothing else.

However, Google decided to block this application. Reason: Impersonation. Areas: title and icon. All I can do is re-upload the application with a different name and a different icon, and hope that Google will not give a new block. So, goodbye dear Klee. :(

This application will continue to work normally. It will receive new promo codes and web events, because it does not require updating the application. But it will not receive updates to the application itself.
If you like this app, you really use it & you want to receive updates - download the re-uploaded version from here - Genshin Helper. 

Unfortunately, I will not be able to disable ads for those who have already supported developer, because I do not have access to the previous bundle.

Best wishes to you, travelers.
